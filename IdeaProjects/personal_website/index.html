Music

Yeah Okay

September 5, 2021

18 songs

28 minutes

This is my first album. Although it's composed entirely of beats, it's more so an album than it is a beat tape due to its structure and composition. I made these beats in my bedroom during the summer of 2021, during quarantine, when I was first getting the hang of producing. I played the bass on 'Discipline', 'Midnight on the Moon', 'Eternal' (the second half of 'Other Planets / Eternal'). I programmed the drums on all tracks except "Tribe's Drums" and 'Traveling'. The beats draw heavily on foreign samples from the 70s, 80s and 90s, including 'The Mail Troika' by Melodia and 'Sihirbaz' by Gökçen Kaynatan. Cover art by me.

Donnie Darko Demos

October 8, 2021

12 songs

26 minutes

In the process of making my first album, I had a lot of outtakes. I was just learning how to record my guitar and bass through my audio interface, and at this point I hadn't picked up my guitar in months. At a lot of these beats are unfinished ideas or melodies that I heard in my head and recorded, so take it with a grain of salt if you hear some poor mixes and masters. My favourite beat, at the time, was 'Celeste', with its fat kicks and almost ethereal feel (that's how I felt it). This demo tape is kind of a mess, but it represents my time surrounding the release of 'Yeah Okay'. Cover art by me (it's just my LEGO mug).

Delight (ft. Jade Russian)

Single

2 minutes

Here we go. My first single. Finally got a voice to my beats. A real good one, too. Jade Russian–with her ever beautiful voice–delivers a stunning performance on this one. By this point, I had a new keyboard with which I'd been practising with for a while. I recorded everything on this one, and Jade delivered everything so quickly and with such passion. Her beautiful harmonies overlay the smooth melody, and my personal favourite part is the bridge. Cover art is by Jade Russian.
